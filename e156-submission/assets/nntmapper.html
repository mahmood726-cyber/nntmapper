NNTMapper v1.0.0

Meta-Analytic Effect → Clinical Decision Converter

Converts pooled meta-analytic effects (OR, RR, HR) into clinician-friendly metrics: Number Needed to Treat, Absolute Risk Reduction, Decision Curves, and Population Impact estimates.

NNT Formulas

  • OR: ARR = CER - CER·OR / (1 - CER + CER·OR); NNT = 1/ARR
  • RR: ARR = CER·(1 - RR); NNT = 1/ARR
  • HR: ARR = CER - (1 - (1-CER)^HR); NNT = 1/ARR

Prediction Intervals

When tau² > 0, the prediction interval for the pooled effect is computed as θ ± t_{k-2,0.975} · √(SE² + tau²), then converted to the NNT scale.

Decision Curves

Net benefit at threshold p_t: NB = (TP/N) - (FP/N)·(p_t/(1-p_t)). Three strategies compared: Treat All, Treat None, and the meta-analytic estimate.

Limitations

  • NNT depends heavily on baseline risk — always specify the target population
  • HR-to-NNT conversion assumes exponential survival (constant hazard)
  • Decision curves use a simplified binary outcome framework
  • Heterogeneity propagation uses normal approximation of the PI
